Breakfast Sausage


  • Ingredients:


    • For the Sausage:

      • 1 pound ground pork
      • 1 teaspoon salt
      • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
      • 1 teaspoon sage
      • 1/2 teaspoon thyme
      • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
      • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

  • Recipe:


    • Mix the Ingredients:

      Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. Mix well until spices are evenly distributed.


    • Form the Sausages:

      Shape the mixture into small patties or rolls, as desired.


    • Cook the Sausages:

      Heat a skillet over medium heat. Cook the sausage patties for 4-5 minutes on each side or until golden brown and fully cooked.


    • Serve:

      Serve hot with eggs, toast, or pancakes.

Cuban Sandwich


  • Ingredients:


    • For the Sandwich:

      • 2 slices Cuban bread
      • 3 ounces roasted pork
      • 2 ounces ham
      • 2 slices Swiss cheese
      • 2-3 pickle slices
      • 1 tablespoon mustard
      • Butter (for toasting)

  • Recipe:


    • Assemble the Sandwich:

      Spread mustard on one side of the bread. Layer roasted pork, ham, Swiss cheese, and pickles, then top with the second slice of bread.


    • Toast the Sandwich:

      Heat a sandwich press or skillet. Butter the outside of the sandwich and cook until golden brown and cheese is melted.


    • Serve:

      Slice and serve hot with plantain chips or a salad.

Hamburger


  • Ingredients:


    • For the Burger Patties:

      • 1 pound ground beef (80% lean)
      • Salt and pepper (to taste)
      • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce

    • For Assembling:

      • 4 burger buns
      • Lettuce leaves
      • Sliced tomatoes
      • Sliced onions
      • Cheese slices (optional)
      • Ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise (as preferred)

  • Recipe:


    • Prepare the Patties:

      In a bowl, mix ground beef, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper. Shape into four equal patties.


    • Cook the Patties:

      Heat a skillet or grill over medium heat. Cook the patties for 3-4 minutes per side or until desired doneness.


    • Assemble the Burgers:

      Toast the burger buns. Place a patty on each bun and layer with lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and cheese. Add condiments as desired.


    • Serve:

      Serve with fries or a side salad.

Lemonade Fried Chicken


  • Ingredients:


    • For the Marinade:

      • 1 cup lemonade
      • 1 teaspoon salt
      • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
      • 1 teaspoon garlic powder

    • For the Coating:

      • 1 cup all-purpose flour
      • 1 teaspoon paprika
      • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
      • Salt and pepper (to taste)

    • Other Ingredients:

      • 4 chicken drumsticks or thighs
      • Vegetable oil (for frying)

  • Recipe:


    • Marinate the Chicken:

      In a bowl, combine lemonade, salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Add the chicken and let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours.


    • Prepare the Coating:

      Mix flour, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper in a shallow dish.


    • Coat the Chicken:

      Remove the chicken from the marinade, letting excess drip off. Dredge the chicken in the flour mixture, coating evenly.


    • Fry the Chicken:

      Heat oil in a deep skillet to 350°F (175°C). Fry the chicken until golden brown and cooked through, about 10-12 minutes.


    • Serve:

      Serve hot with coleslaw or mashed potatoes.

Old-Fashioned Apple Pie


  • Ingredients:


    • For the Crust:

      • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
      • 1 teaspoon salt
      • 1 tablespoon sugar
      • 1 cup unsalted butter (cold, diced)
      • 6-8 tablespoons ice water

    • For the Filling:

      • 6-7 apples (peeled, cored, and sliced)
      • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
      • 1/4 cup brown sugar
      • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
      • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
      • 2 tablespoons flour
      • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

  • Recipe:


    • Prepare the Crust:

      Mix flour, salt, and sugar. Add butter and mix until crumbly. Gradually add ice water and form a dough. Divide into two, wrap, and chill.


    • Prepare the Filling:

      In a large bowl, mix apple slices, sugars, cinnamon, nutmeg, flour, and lemon juice.


    • Assemble the Pie:

      Roll out one dough and place it in a pie dish. Add the filling. Roll out the second dough and cover the pie, sealing the edges.


    • Bake:

      Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Bake the pie for 50-60 minutes or until golden brown.


    • Serve:

      Cool slightly before serving with whipped cream or ice cream.
