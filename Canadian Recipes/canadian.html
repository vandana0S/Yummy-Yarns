Bannock


  • Ingredients:


    • Main Ingredients:

      • 2 cups all-purpose flour
      • 1 tablespoon baking powder
      • 1/2 teaspoon salt
      • 3 tablespoons butter (melted)
      • 3/4 cup water or milk

  • Recipe:


    • Mix the Dough:

      In a bowl, combine flour, baking powder, and salt. Stir in melted butter and water or milk to form a soft dough.


    • Shape the Dough:

      Turn the dough onto a floured surface and knead lightly. Pat into a round disk about 1/2 inch thick.


    • Cook the Bannock:

      Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat. Place the dough in the skillet and cook for 10-12 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through.


    • Serve:

      Serve warm with butter, jam, or honey.

Lobster Rolls


  • Ingredients:


    • Main Ingredients:

      • 1 pound cooked lobster meat (chopped)
      • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
      • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
      • 2 tablespoons celery (finely chopped)
      • Salt and pepper (to taste)
      • 4 hot dog buns
      • 2 tablespoons butter (melted)

  • Recipe:


    • Prepare the Filling:

      In a bowl, mix lobster meat, mayonnaise, lemon juice, celery, salt, and pepper. Chill for 15 minutes.


    • Toast the Buns:

      Brush the inside of the buns with melted butter and toast them in a skillet until golden.


    • Assemble the Rolls:

      Fill each bun with the lobster mixture. Serve immediately.

Maple Syrup


Note: Maple syrup is typically made from sap collected from maple trees, boiled down into a concentrated syrup. This is a simplified home recipe.


  • Ingredients:


    • Main Ingredients:

      • 2 cups granulated sugar
      • 1 cup brown sugar
      • 1 cup water
      • 1 teaspoon maple extract

  • Recipe:


    • Boil the Ingredients:

      In a saucepan, combine sugars and water. Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring until the sugars dissolve.


    • Add Flavor:

      Remove from heat and stir in maple extract.


    • Store and Serve:

      Allow the syrup to cool and store in an airtight container. Serve over pancakes, waffles, or desserts.

Peameal Bacon Sandwiches


  • Ingredients:


    • Main Ingredients:

      • 1 pound peameal bacon (sliced)
      • 4 sandwich rolls
      • 2 tablespoons mustard
      • Optional toppings: lettuce, tomato, cheese

  • Recipe:


    • Cook the Bacon:

      Heat a skillet over medium heat. Cook the peameal bacon slices until golden brown on both sides.


    • Toast the Rolls:

      Lightly toast the sandwich rolls in the skillet or toaster.


    • Assemble the Sandwich:

      Spread mustard on the rolls. Add peameal bacon slices and any optional toppings.

    • Serve:

      Serve warm with your favorite side dish.

Saskatoon Berries (Jam Recipe)


  • Ingredients:


    • Main Ingredients:

      • 4 cups Saskatoon berries
      • 2 cups sugar
      • 1/4 cup lemon juice

  • Recipe:


    • Cook the Berries:

      In a saucepan, combine Saskatoon berries, sugar, and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring, until the berries release their juice and the mixture thickens (about 15-20 minutes).


    • Store and Serve:

      Transfer to sterilized jars and seal. Use as a spread for bread or desserts.
