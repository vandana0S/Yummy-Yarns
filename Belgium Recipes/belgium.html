Boulets à la Liégeoise (Belgian Meatballs in Sauce)


  • Ingredients:


    • For the Meatballs:

      • 500 g ground beef and pork
      • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
      • 1 egg
      • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
      • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
      • Salt and pepper (to taste)

    • For the Sauce:

      • 2 onions (sliced)
      • 2 tablespoons butter
      • 1/2 cup apple or pear syrup
      • 1 cup beef stock
      • 1 tablespoon mustard
      • Salt and pepper (to taste)

  • Recipe:


    • Prepare the Meatballs:

      Mix ground meat, onion, egg, breadcrumbs, thyme, salt, and pepper. Form into balls and refrigerate for 30 minutes.


    • Cook the Meatballs:

      In a skillet, brown the meatballs on all sides. Remove and set aside.


    • Prepare the Sauce:

      In the same skillet, sauté onions in butter until caramelized. Add apple syrup, beef stock, mustard, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.


    • Combine:

      Add the meatballs back to the skillet and simmer for 20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.


    • Serve:

      Serve hot with fries or mashed potatoes.

Belgian Fries (Frites)


  • Ingredients:


    • Main Ingredients:

      • 6 large potatoes
      • Vegetable oil (for frying)
      • Salt (to taste)

  • Recipe:


    • Prepare the Potatoes:

      Peel the potatoes and cut them into even strips (about 1/4 inch thick). Rinse in cold water to remove excess starch.


    • First Fry:

      Heat oil in a deep fryer or heavy-bottomed pot to 150°C (300°F). Fry the potato strips in batches for 5-7 minutes until soft but not browned. Drain on paper towels.


    • Second Fry:

      Increase the oil temperature to 190°C (375°F). Fry the potatoes again in batches until golden and crispy, about 2-3 minutes. Drain and sprinkle with salt.


    • Serve:

      Serve hot with mayonnaise, ketchup, or your favorite dipping sauce.

Shrimp Croquettes (Croquettes aux Crevettes)


  • Ingredients:


    • For the Croquettes:

      • 2 cups shrimp (cooked and finely chopped)
      • 2 tablespoons butter
      • 1/4 cup flour
      • 1 cup milk
      • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
      • Salt and pepper (to taste)
      • 2 eggs (beaten)
      • 1 cup breadcrumbs
      • Vegetable oil (for frying)

  • Recipe:


    • Prepare the Filling:

      In a pan, melt butter and stir in the flour to form a roux. Gradually add milk, stirring constantly, until smooth and thickened. Add chopped shrimp, nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Cool the mixture and shape into small logs.


    • Coat the Croquettes:

      Dip each croquette into beaten eggs, then roll in breadcrumbs to coat evenly.


    • Fry the Croquettes:

      Heat oil in a fryer or pan to 180°C (350°F). Fry croquettes until golden brown and crispy, about 2-3 minutes. Drain on paper towels.


    • Serve:

      Serve warm with lemon wedges or tartar sauce.

Stoemp (Mashed Potatoes with Vegetables)


  • Ingredients:


    • Main Ingredients:

      • 1 kg potatoes (peeled and cubed)
      • 2 carrots (chopped)
      • 1 leek (sliced)
      • 1/4 cup butter
      • 1/4 cup cream
      • Salt and pepper (to taste)

  • Recipe:


    • Boil the Vegetables:

      In a pot, boil potatoes, carrots, and leek together in salted water until tender, about 15-20 minutes.


    • Mash the Mixture:

      Drain the vegetables and mash them together in the pot. Add butter and cream, stirring to combine.


    • Season and Serve:

      Add salt and pepper to taste. Serve warm as a side dish with sausages or meat.

Waterzooi (Belgian Stew)


  • Ingredients:


    • Main Ingredients:

      • 1 whole chicken (cut into pieces) or 500 g fish fillets
      • 2 carrots (sliced)
      • 2 leeks (sliced)
      • 1 onion (chopped)
      • 4 potatoes (cubed)
      • 1 cup cream
      • 4 cups chicken or fish stock
      • 2 tablespoons butter
      • Salt and pepper (to taste)

  • Recipe:


    • Cook the Chicken or Fish:

      In a large pot, melt butter and lightly sauté chicken pieces or fish fillets. Remove and set aside.


    • Prepare the Vegetables:

      In the same pot, add carrots, leeks, onion, and potatoes. Sauté for 5 minutes, then add the stock. Simmer until the vegetables are tender.


    • Combine and Finish:

      Return the chicken or fish to the pot. Stir in cream, season with salt and pepper, and simmer for 10 more minutes.


    • Serve:

      Serve hot with crusty bread or over rice.
