Paneer Tikka Receipe

    Ingrediants:



    • 250g paneer, cut into cubes
    • 1 cup yogurt
    • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
    • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala
    • Salt to taste
    • 1 tablespoon oil
    • Lemon wedges and onion rings for garnish

    Instructions:

    1. Mix yogurt with ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt.
    2. Marinate paneer cubes in this mixture for 1-2 hours.
    3. Thread paneer onto skewers and grill or bake until golden brown.
    4. Serve hot with lemon wedges and onion rings.
