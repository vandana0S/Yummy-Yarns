Adobo


  • Ingredients:


    • Main Ingredients:

      • 1 kg chicken or pork (cut into serving pieces)
      • 1/2 cup soy sauce
      • 1/2 cup vinegar
      • 1 head garlic (minced)
      • 2 bay leaves
      • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
      • 1 cup water
      • 2 tablespoons cooking oil

  • Recipe:


    • Marinate the Meat:

      Combine chicken or pork, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns in a bowl. Marinate for 30 minutes to 1 hour.


    • Sauté and Cook:

      Heat oil in a pot and sauté the marinated meat until lightly browned. Add the marinade and water, then bring to a boil.


    • Simmer:

      Lower the heat and simmer until the meat is tender and the sauce thickens.


    • Serve:

      Serve hot with steamed rice.

Bibingka


  • Ingredients:


    • Main Ingredients:

      • 1 cup rice flour
      • 1 cup coconut milk
      • 1/2 cup sugar
      • 2 eggs
      • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
      • Banana leaves (optional, for lining)
      • Grated cheese or salted egg slices (for topping)

  • Recipe:


    • Prepare the Batter:

      Mix rice flour, coconut milk, sugar, eggs, and baking powder in a bowl until smooth.


    • Line the Pan:

      Line a baking pan or ramekin with banana leaves (optional) and pour in the batter.


    • Bake:

      Bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.


    • Add Toppings:

      Top with grated cheese or salted egg slices during the last few minutes of baking.


    • Serve:

      Serve warm with butter or grated coconut on the side.

Cassava Cake


  • Ingredients:


    • Main Ingredients:

      • 2 cups grated cassava
      • 1 cup coconut milk
      • 1/2 cup condensed milk
      • 2 eggs
      • 1/2 cup sugar
      • 1/4 cup butter (melted)
      • Grated cheese (for topping)

  • Recipe:


    • Prepare the Batter:

      In a bowl, combine grated cassava, coconut milk, condensed milk, eggs, sugar, and melted butter. Mix well.


    • Pour into Pan:

      Pour the mixture into a greased baking dish.


    • Bake:

      Bake in a preheated oven at 350°F (175°C) for 45-50 minutes or until the top is golden and the cake is set.


    • Add Cheese:

      Sprinkle grated cheese on top and return to the oven for 5 minutes to melt.


    • Serve:

      Let cool before slicing. Serve as a dessert or snack.

Kare-Kare


  • Ingredients:


    • Main Ingredients:

      • 1 kg oxtail or beef (cut into chunks)
      • 1 cup peanut butter
      • 1/4 cup annatto oil (optional)
      • 1/2 cup rice flour (toasted)
      • 2 cups water or beef stock
      • Eggplant, bok choy, and string beans (sliced)
      • Salt and pepper (to taste)
      • Shrimp paste (for serving)

  • Recipe:


    • Cook the Meat:

      Boil the oxtail or beef until tender. Set aside the meat and stock.


    • Prepare the Sauce:

      In a pot, heat annatto oil. Add peanut butter, rice flour, and water or stock. Stir until smooth and thickened.


    • Add Vegetables:

      Add the vegetables and simmer until tender.


    • Combine:

      Add the cooked meat to the pot. Season with salt and pepper.


    • Serve:

      Serve hot with steamed rice and shrimp paste on the side.

Lumpia


  • Ingredients:


    • Main Ingredients:

      • 1 lb ground pork or chicken
      • 1 cup chopped vegetables (carrots, cabbage, green beans)
      • 1/2 cup onion (finely chopped)
      • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
      • 1 egg
      • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
      • 1 teaspoon salt
      • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
      • Spring roll wrappers
      • Cooking oil (for frying)

  • Recipe:


    • Prepare the Filling:

      Mix the ground meat, vegetables, onion, garlic, egg, soy sauce, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Combine thoroughly.


    • Wrap the Lumpia:

      Place a spoonful of the filling onto a spring roll wrapper. Roll tightly, folding in the sides, and seal the edge with water.


    • Fry:

      Heat oil in a pan and fry the lumpia until golden brown. Drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.


    • Serve:

      Serve hot with sweet chili sauce or vinegar dip.
