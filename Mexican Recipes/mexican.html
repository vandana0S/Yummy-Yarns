Ceviche (Peruvian Seafood Dish)


  • Ingredients:


    • Main Ingredients:

      • 500 g fresh white fish fillet (sea bass or tilapia, cubed)
      • 1 cup lime juice (freshly squeezed)
      • 1 red onion (thinly sliced)
      • 1-2 chili peppers (thinly sliced, optional)
      • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro (chopped)
      • Salt (to taste)
      • 1-2 sweet potatoes (boiled and sliced, for serving)
      • Corn (boiled, for serving)

  • Recipe:


    • Prepare the Fish:

      Cut the fish into small cubes and place in a bowl.


    • Marinate:

      Pour the lime juice over the fish. Add sliced onions and chili peppers. Let it marinate in the refrigerator for 20-30 minutes until the fish turns opaque.


    • Season:

      Add chopped cilantro and salt to taste. Mix gently.


    • Serve:

      Plate the ceviche with sweet potatoes and corn on the side. Serve immediately.

Guacamole (Mexican Avocado Dip)


  • Ingredients:


    • Main Ingredients:

      • 3 ripe avocados
      • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
      • 1-2 tomatoes (diced)
      • 1-2 chili peppers (finely chopped)
      • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
      • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro (chopped)
      • Salt (to taste)

  • Recipe:


    • Prepare the Avocados:

      Cut the avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop the flesh into a bowl. Mash to your desired consistency.


    • Mix Ingredients:

      Add chopped onion, tomatoes, chili peppers, lime juice, cilantro, and salt. Stir gently to combine.


    • Adjust Seasoning:

      Taste and adjust salt or lime juice as needed.


    • Serve:

      Serve immediately with tortilla chips or as a side to Mexican dishes.

Menudo (Mexican Stew)


  • Ingredients:


    • Main Ingredients:

      • 1 kg beef tripe (cleaned and cut into pieces)
      • 500 g pork or beef (cubed)
      • 2 liters water
      • 1 cup hominy (cooked)
      • 2 tablespoons chili powder
      • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
      • 1 onion (diced)
      • Salt and pepper (to taste)
      • Fresh lime wedges (for serving)

  • Recipe:


    • Cook the Meat:

      In a large pot, combine tripe, beef, water, garlic, and onion. Simmer for 2-3 hours until tender.


    • Add Ingredients:

      Stir in hominy and chili powder. Cook for another 30 minutes.


    • Season:

      Add salt and pepper to taste.


    • Serve:

      Ladle the stew into bowls and serve with lime wedges, chopped onions, and cilantro as garnishes.

Quesadillas (Mexican Grilled Cheese Dish)


  • Ingredients:


    • Main Ingredients:

      • 8 large flour tortillas
      • 2 cups shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or Mexican blend)
      • 1 cup cooked chicken, beef, or vegetables (optional)
      • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro (chopped)
      • Butter or oil (for cooking)
      • Salsa, guacamole, or sour cream (for serving)

  • Recipe:


    • Prepare the Filling:

      In a bowl, mix shredded cheese with cooked chicken, beef, or vegetables if using. Add chopped cilantro.


    • Assemble the Quesadillas:

      Lay a tortilla flat and spread the cheese mixture evenly on half of it. Fold the tortilla in half.


    • Cook the Quesadillas:

      Heat a skillet over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or oil. Place the folded tortilla in the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes per side until golden brown and the cheese is melted.


    • Serve:

      Cut into wedges and serve hot with salsa, guacamole, or sour cream.
